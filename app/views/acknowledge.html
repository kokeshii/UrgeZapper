Acknowledge your Urge

To zap your urge, you must first acknowledge it.

Follow these steps:

  • Accept that you're having the urge

    By accept, we don't mean that you have to agree you're having the urge. You just need to accept the fact that the thought of having the urge is crossing your mind.

  • Feel the urge for 60 seconds

    Press the big red button below and close your eyes. Breathe slowly and deeply, and let the thought of engaging on the urge flood your mind. Then, bring to mind the reasons why you don't want to indulge. When you hear the sound of the bell, open your eyes.

  • Wait it Out

    After feeling the urge to {{ urgeName }}, you will need to wait it out. 99% of people who first feel and then wait their urge to {{ urgeName }}, find themselves totally, if not majorly defused from their urge.
