Take Your Willpower to the next Level

The original idea of UrgeZapper came from chapter 7 of the life-changing book "The Willpower Instinct". Be sure to check it out for even more techniques to train your brain and enhance your willpower to the extreme!

We're Animals, After All

Our primitive brains are wired to crave immediate satisfaction, be it in the form of food, sex, or other kinds of pleasurable activity.

When we establish a mandatory 10-minute period between urge and indulging, our brain sees the action as a future reward, making it less desirable.

Urges Are Temporary

If you indulge in an urge immediately after you get it, you don't know that even the strongest urges are temporary, and last between 1 to 10 minutes.

It Works like Magic

99% of the people that have used this technique, discover that the urge is gone by the time or even before the timer runs out.

"Never Again" Is Just Too Harsh

Instead of trying to quit a bad habit cold turkey, strengthen your willpower by putting a delay between the time you have the urge and indulging. If you keep doing it you will eventually break the habit.

Willpower Is like a Muscle

The more you work it, the stronger it becomes. If you use UrgeZapper every time you have an urge to {{ urgeName }} or any other kind of urge, you will be better at recognizing and resisting it.
